Our Services

Our primary focus is to provide tank cleaning, industrial cleaning, engineering services and procurement services to major corporations, especially in the oil and gas sector.

SHIP ENGINE ROOM AND TANKS CLEANING

Best technology available to clean tanks, pipes, engine rooms and to remove marine growth in ships & vessels.

CLEANING OF HEAVY MACHINERY

We have mobile units to carry out any work of cleaning of heavy machinery and port equipments. Cleaning on-site and off-site.

TREATMENT OF WASH WATER

We have vacuum trucks, tanker trailers and qualified personnel to perform various pumping jobs on ships.

CARGO HEATING AND ACCOMMODATIONS

With several high-efficiency heating units and our trained staff, we meet all your heating needs.

FRESH WATER DELIVERY

We supply ships with fresh water using our 38,000-liters tanker trucks.

SHIP SUPPLIES & SHUTTLE SERVICE

Delivery alongside or anchorage. We can supply ships 24/7. All these services are provided at deck or at anchorage.
